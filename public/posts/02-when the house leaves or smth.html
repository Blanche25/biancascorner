02-when the house leaves or smth

5th jan 2025

so! i've just finished reading House of Leaves by Mark Z. Danielewski and i wanted to say a couple things about it so here we go!


it's an incredibly confusing book, and intentionally so. in many parts it feels like its actively trying to make you stop reading and do literally anything else and i absolutely love it. even though my attention span is ass it still managed to keep me interested the whole way through and i managed to finish it in like a week which is a lot less than i expected.
‮ also what's up with Johnny? the book never really tells you what's really happening with him in any way whatsoever. i got to the end still trying to figure out what the fuck i just read. also why does he like to write about sex so much? like damn bro chill jesus christ.
every time i was curious to see what inflated arguments it would pull out of its ass to bore you to death. and sometimes it's not even worthless! like it makes you think it is but then somehow it comes back? like without spoiling too much there's a part in which it goes into an analysis of the echo in every sense possible but then you find out it's a theme that returns multiple times!
pretty crazy book, though definitely not for everyone, i can see how some people would absolutely hate it.
i know absolutely nothing about the minotaur. 700 pages later and i've no idea what it is
i also didn't take any notes while reading, though sometimes i was tempted and i know many people did (can't blame them i mean if i were to try and analyze this i would have a stroke). definitely one of the weirdest books i've ever read. absolutely worth it though. i'm also kinda disappointed i don't have one of those editions with the cover slightly smaller than the book itself.


...so yeah overall pretty good book, 8/8, might be one of my favorites.
